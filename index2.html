  • Manhwa are Korean comics or graphic novels, similar to Japanese manga and Chinese manhua.

  • They are typically read from left to right (like Western comics) and are known for their vibrant artwork and diverse storylines.

    Key Features of Manhwa:
  • Origin: They are created in South Korea.
  • Reading Direction: Left to right.
  • Formats: They can be found in print or as webtoons (digital comics).
  • Genres: Include romance, fantasy, action, horror, comedy, and more.
  • Art Style: Usually colorful and detailed, with a modern look.
