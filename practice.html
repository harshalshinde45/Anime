THESE ARE SOME WEBSITES TO VISIT

1. H! ANIME

HiAnime.to is a website that streams anime content, including episodes and movies. However, it hosts copyrighted content without proper licensing, making it an illegal streaming platform.

2. Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll is a legal streaming service that specializes in anime, manga, and Asian dramas. It offers both free and premium subscription options.

3. Aniwatch

Aniwatch was a popular website offering free streaming of anime content with English subtitles and dubs.
