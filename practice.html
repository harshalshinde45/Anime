2. Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll is a streaming service that specializes in anime, manga, and Asian dramas. It offers a vast library of anime series, including popular titles like *Attack on Titan*, *One Piece*, *My Hero Academia*, and many more. Crunchyroll also provides simulcasts, which allow users to watch new episodes shortly after they air in Japan. In addition to streaming, Crunchyroll offers digital manga, news related to anime and pop culture, and even merchandise. It has both free (ad-supported) and premium subscription options, with the premium version offering ad-free viewing, early access to new episodes, and the ability to download content for offline viewing.
